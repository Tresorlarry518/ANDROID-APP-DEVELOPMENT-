# ANDROID-APP-DEVELOPMENT-
I researched existing carbon tracking solutions that banks use like Cogo and Ecolytiq, but noticed they're all designed for general customers. I noticed that no one has made a version specifically for students. So I designed the CarbonTrack app with features students actually need: campus comparisons, budget-friendly tips, and peer competition.

We track our steps, our screen time, and our budgets on here — why not track our carbon footprint too? That’s what CarbonTrack does: it’s like Fitbit but for your carbon emissions.

Carbon track 
My project is about how much pollution your lifestyle creates and makes it visible so you can reduce it in smart ways

Carbon footprint = the invisible pollution tied to what you buy, eat, and do.

1. **Problem:** 
Students want to be sustainable but don't know their impact

2. **Solution:**
 CarbonTrack – automatic carbon tracking made for student life

3. **Revenue Model:** 
Universities pay your a fees to my app so students can use it for free

4. **Impact:** 
Students reduces footprint, campuses get greener, and I get more income to do further improvements of the app


As the founder, I created CarbonTrack App. bringing this idea to market for Universities to pay a small fee per student, which would fund operations and growth while keeping the app free for students to use.
